Top Classical Composers

Ludwig van Beethoven

A revolutionary German composer whose works, like the 9th Symphony, have shaped classical music forever.

Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

A prolific and influential composer of the Classical era, known for his symphonies, operas, and concertos.

Johann Sebastian Bach

A master of Baroque music, Bach's compositions are celebrated for their intellectual depth and beauty.

Frédéric Chopin

A Polish composer and virtuoso pianist, Chopin is renowned for his expressive piano works and romantic style.
